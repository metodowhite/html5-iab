PAP (Premium Accessories by Pyk) is a leather goods company that features handmade products that are produced exclusively in Sweden. We believe that timeless design is always modern and that true beauty gets better with age. However, all our products are made to facilitate, complement and improve contemporary lifestyles.

Our bags and covers are tailored to protect and carry the latest personal technology, lending a sense of style and character to your phones and computers at the same time as you shield them from wear and tear. Read the full story.

PAP products are distributed and sold all over the world, from New York to Seoul, as well as in out on online store. You can use our store locator to find the store closest to you. If you can not find a store close to you, feel free to contact us, or order directly from our online store. Click to find a store.

"My inspiration for PAP was to create a product that was an antidote to the cold and impersonal feeling of modern technology. I wanted the warm and organic sensation of leather to replace the cold feeling of a laptop aluminum shell", says Ulf Pyk, Head of design. Read the full story.

As a small company we are constantly on the road, visiting manufacturers, resellers and trade shows. Trying to travel light, one thing we never leave behind is our phones. On Instagram you can follow us on our journeys and get a glimpse of what our everyday life looks like. Get a snapshot of PAP.
